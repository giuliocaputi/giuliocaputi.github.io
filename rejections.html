2024–25 Rejections in Quantitative Finance Applications


This is a collection of all the rejections I received while applying for quant finance jobs during my Master’s at Oxford.

I did not have any experience in quant finance applications, but I did spend a couple of (necessary) months over the summer preparing full-time for interviews.

My CV used a standard LaTeX template (example here), and included Oxford (MSc in Statistics), Bocconi (BSc in Computer Science and Economics), GeorgiaTech (exchange semester), Amazon (data science internship), and Deloitte (cyber security internship).

Virtually every other student applying to these jobs encountered the same pattern: the overwhelming majority of applications were either rejected at the CV screening stage or received no response, even after multiple submissions to the same role or company, spaced roughly 3 months apart.

There are plenty of people out there who truly need to see this. I hope some of them do, and that it helps them feel a little better.
