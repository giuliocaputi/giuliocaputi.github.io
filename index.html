Essentials About Me

Sparing you clichés like "extremely motivated", "hardworking", "data-driven", et cetera, here are some essentials about me:

Academics

Currently pursuing an MSc in Statistics at the University of Oxford. Graduated Cum Laude with a BSc in Computer Science and Economics from Bocconi University. Completed an exchange semester at the Georgia Institute of Technology.

Industry

Currently aiming for a job in quant finance. Completed a 6-month data science internship at Amazon Luxembourg and a 3-month cybersecurity internship at Deloitte Italy, both while studying.

Extracurriculars

Co-founded and led Bocconi Students for Machine Learning, and participated in several other university associations. I'm also a member of Nova and LeadTheFuture, which are selective talent networks.

Research Interests

Both at Amazon and in my undergraduate thesis, I explored the application of machine learning and advanced statistical techniques (mainly Bayesian Optimisation) to optimise database systems, which are complex, expensive-to evaluate, black-box functions. Additionally, I employed network science to model web page interconnectivity at Bocconi Students for Machine Learning.
